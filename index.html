How It Works
Three steps. Sixty seconds.
No training. No onboarding calls. If you can tap a checkbox, you can use this.
01
☑️
Pick Your Line Items
Open a trade category — Roofing, Electrical, Plumbing, whatever the job calls for. Check the items that apply. Enter quantity and your rate. The math handles itself.
02
📋
Add Details & Send
Drop in client info, job address, notes, even photos of the work area. Hit Send to email it directly, or Save as PDF to print. Your quote looks professional either way.

Invoice & Get Paid
When the client accepts, convert to an invoice in one click. Track payment status. Connect Stripe so they can pay by card. You see the money in your bank — not a "pending" screen.
